ReefSMP Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we use and protect data while running ReefSMP services.

Last updated:

1) How We Use Your Data

We may use data to:

  • Operate the Minecraft server and related services
  • Improve performance, balance, gameplay features, and stability
  • Detect and prevent abuse, cheating, fraud, and rule violations
  • Provide support and investigate technical issues

We may use your data to make the server run better (optimization, bug fixing, and quality improvements).

2) Data Retention

We keep data as long as necessary to operate the service, enforce rules, and meet legal or security needs. In general, data is retained while your player account is active.

Some logs may be retained longer when required for security, abuse prevention, or dispute handling.

3) Store Purchases, No Refunds, and Product Changes

All purchases are final and non-refundable. By purchasing from our store, you agree that we do not provide refunds.

We may update the store at any time, including:

  • Changing store package contents, pricing, and availability
  • Changing any rank name, crate name, or reward name at any time

These changes may happen with or without notice.

4) Rules, Abuse, and Enforcement

Any breaking of the rules will result in punishment. Punishments may include warnings, mutes, temporary bans, permanent bans, account wipes, or other actions decided by staff.

Examples of serious violations include (but are not limited to):

  • DDoSing or attempting to disrupt our servers or services
  • Leaking someone’s personal information (doxxing)
  • Creating or spreading fake information about any staff members
  • Threats, harassment, or encouraging harm
  • Cheating, exploiting, or abusing bugs

We may take action with or without notice depending on severity, and we may cooperate with service providers or authorities when required.

5) Changes to This Policy

We have the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time, with or without notice. If you continue using ReefSMP after changes are published, you agree to the updated policy.

For important updates, we may announce them on Discord or the website, but we are not required to do so.

6) Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your stored data
  • Correction of incorrect data
  • Deletion of data (when possible)
  • Transfer/export of data (when possible)

To make a request, open a support ticket in our Discord: discord.gg/ReefNetwork.

7) What We Collect

We may collect and store data required to operate ReefSMP and provide support, including:

  • IP address and basic connection information
  • In-game statistics and gameplay data
  • Store transactions and purchase history
  • Support logs (tickets, messages, moderation notes)

This data is stored in a secure database and used only for running and improving the service.

9) Third-Party Services

ReefSMP uses third-party services to operate our network and store. These may include hosting providers, payment processors (such as Tebex), Discord, Cloudflare, analytics services, and embedded content providers.

These third-party providers may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. ReefSMP is not responsible for how third-party services handle your data.

10) Payments & Taxes

All purchases are final and non-refundable.

Store purchases may include processing fees, taxes, or currency conversion fees applied by third-party payment providers. ReefSMP does not control these fees and cannot refund third-party charges.

11) Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no system is 100% secure. By using our services, you acknowledge that ReefSMP cannot guarantee absolute security of stored data.

12) Age Requirement

By using ReefSMP services, you confirm that you are at least 13 years old, or that you have permission from a parent or legal guardian.

If you do not meet this requirement, you must not use our services.

13) Limitation of Liability

ReefSMP is not responsible for data loss, account loss, in-game item loss, service interruptions, technical failures, or damages resulting from the use of our services, unless caused by proven internal system failure.

ReefSMP is also not responsible for any data loss, item loss, or progress loss that may occur as a result of server restarts, maintenance, updates, or unexpected shutdowns.

Use of the server and store is at your own risk.

14) Appeals & Punishment Clarification

All moderation decisions are made at the discretion of ReefSMP staff. Punishments may include warnings, mutes, temporary bans, permanent bans, account wipes, or other restrictions.

ReefSMP staff are not required to provide evidence or justification for any punishment issued.

Appeals may be submitted through our official Discord. Abuse of the appeal system or repeated false appeals may result in further punishment. Final decisions may be reviewed by senior staff.
