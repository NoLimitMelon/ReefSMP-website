🗿 About TotemRush

TotemRush is a competitive Minecraft network focused on survival, events, and skill-based gameplay. We aim to provide a fair, .net, and high-quality experience for both casual and competitive players.

Whether you enjoy building, PvP, grinding, or events, TotemRush offers a place for every playstyle.

⚔️ Gameplay

  • Survival SMP with a player-driven economy
  • Competitive PvP and skill-based combat
  • Regular events with rewards and prizes
  • Balanced gameplay with active moderation

👥 Community & Vision

TotemRush is built around a strong and active community. We focus on fair play, transparency, and long-term development.

Our goal is to keep TotemRush fresh with events, updates, and features, without unnecessary delays.

TotemRush is constantly evolving. For announcements, updates, and full information, make sure to join our Discord server.

Server Rules

By playing on TotemRush, you agree to follow these rules at all times.

📜 General Rules

  • No abusing bugs or glitches. This may result in an account wipe.
  • Do not impersonate staff members.
  • All store purchases are final and non-refundable.
  • No attempted or actual item duplication.
  • Teaming with a bug abuser or glitcher will result in punishment for the entire team.
  • When a new giveaway is active, previous giveaways are no longer redeemable.
  • Do not find, share, or use the world seed.
  • Do not discuss or promote other SMP servers, including in your status.
  • Rank rewards and names may be changed at any time.
  • Do not lie to staff members under any circumstances.
  • Absolutely no doxxing, swatting, or DDoS attacks.
  • All bugs and glitches must be reported via Discord.
  • item-based scamming is allowed
  • Real-money scamming is not allowed. Exchanging in-game items or currency for store purchases is allowed at your own risk.
  • Be respectful to staff members at all times.
  • Ban evasion is strictly prohibited.

🖥️ Client Rules

  • No hacked or modified clients.
  • No auto-reconnect mods.
  • No movement-altering mods (speed, fly, etc.).
  • No inventory-related mods.
  • No health, damage, or radar indicators.
  • No X-ray, ESP, or similar tools.
  • No freecam usage.
  • No auto-placement tools.
  • No macros, auto-clickers, or scripts.
  • Duping is not allowed, even pretending to dupe may result in punishment.

💬 Chat Rules

  • Do not ask staff or owners to review staff or developer applications.
  • Do not ask staff for keyalls or in-game items.
  • Do not ask for staff positions.
  • No spamming, flooding, or chat riots.
  • No harassment, bullying, or abuse.
  • No sharing personal information (doxxing).
  • No advertising or self-promotion, including other SMPs or DMs.
  • No racism, hate speech, or discriminatory remarks.
  • No selling or trading for real money.
  • No death threats or encouragement of self-harm.

These rules may change at any time. By playing on this server or joining the discord, you agree to all rules listed above and to Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. This page may not always be up to date., Join the discord to get n up-to-date version
